How to Make the Best Dessert

Description

Light and fluffy, the perfect treat to end your meal. Glazed strawberries, angel food cake, and a sweet creamy topping are layered in this colorful, chilled dessert.

Ingredients:

  • Angel Food Cake
  • Cream Cheese
  • White Sugar
  • Whipped Topping
  • Fresh Strawberries
  • Strawberry Glaze

Steps:

  1. Crumble or cut cake into bite-sized pieces. Arrange in a 9x13-inch dish; press cake down to form a layer.
  2. Beat cream cheese and sugar in a medium bowl until light and fluffy. Fold in whipped topping; spread cream cheese mixture over cake layer.
  3. Combine strawberries and glaze in a bowl until strawberries are evenly coated. Spread over cream cheese layer. Chill until serving.
